<>----COOLDOWN----<>
cooldownLength - the length of time the player is on cooldown for (base)
cancelOnCooldown - should hits while on cooldown be cancelled? (otherwise damage will be reduced)
immunityTicks - length of time the damaged player cannot be hit for

<>---KNOCKBACK----<>
kbX - the horizontal multiplier
kbY - the vertical multiplier

<>-----COMBO------<>
comboPeriod - the duration of ticks where extra hits will be counted as a combo
            - if set to less than the cooldownLength (or -1), it will be impossible to combo
comboX - the horizontal multiplier
comboY - the vertical multiplier

<>-----POTS------<>
potX - the horizontal multiplier
potY - the vertical multiplier
